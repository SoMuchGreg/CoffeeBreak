# Signup History — Karazhan / Gruul / Magtheridon

Scope-restricted counterpart of `signup-history.md`. Counts only signups from sets whose content is **exclusively** Karazhan, Gruul's Lair, or Magtheridon's Lair — i.e., all current TBC sets in `sets/`. Old-world sets (ZG/AQ20/Ony, 2026-01-07 through 2026-02-01) are excluded in full.

For counting rules, canonical-name handling, table structure, sort order, sub-table placement, rename/leave handling, and maintenance triggers, see **`signup-history.md`**. Everything there applies identically here — the only difference is which sets feed the counts.

## Scope

- **Included:** the 18 TBC sets in `sets/` (Sunday Karazhan, Wednesday Karazhan, Monday or Sunday or Wednesday Gruul+Mag), from `2026-02-22-sun-karazhan.md` onward. Whether the raid actually cleared, partially cleared, or was cancelled does not matter — any set file in this scope with a `## Signups (from Discord)` section counts.
- **Excluded:** the 7 old-world sets (`2026-01-07-wed-old-world.md` through `2026-02-01-sun-old-world.md`). Those were signup-only historical records of ZG/AQ20/Ony runs and are intentionally out of scope here.

## Maintenance

Update on the same trigger as `signup-history.md`, but only for sets that fall within the **Scope** above. See `reference/file-operations-manual.md` Step 4 for the canonical trigger and delta logic.
